# Peloton Interactive, Inc. — Form 8-K
**Date of Report:** June 6, 2022

## Item 5.02 — Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers

### CFO Transition
On June 6, 2022, the Board of Directors appointed **Elizabeth Coddington as Chief Financial
Officer**, to succeed **Jill Woodworth**, who is stepping down from that role, effective June
13, 2022.

Ms. Coddington, 45, brings more than 20 years of experience in financial planning and
operational finance. She most recently served as Vice President of Finance for Amazon Web
Services (January 2021 to June 2022). Prior to Amazon, she served as CFO of Adara, Inc., VP
Finance and CFO at Walmart.com, and VP Financial Planning and Analysis at Netflix, Inc.

Under her Offer Letter, Ms. Coddington is eligible for: (i) an annual base salary of
$1,000,000, (ii) an equity award valued at $9,000,000 on the date of grant, (iii) up to
$150,000 relocation reimbursement.

### Transition Agreement
In connection with Ms. Woodworth's employment separation, the Company entered into a
transition and consulting agreement with Ms. Woodworth on June 6, 2022. She will provide
consulting services through September 13, 2022, supporting the company in preparation to
report its fiscal year 2022 financial results. **Ms. Woodworth's departure is not the result of
any disagreement with the Company on any subject, including its operations, policies or
practices.**
