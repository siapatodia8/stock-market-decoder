# Peloton Interactive, Inc. — Form 8-K
**Date of Report:** February 5, 2022

## Item 5.02 — Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers

### Director Changes
On February 5, 2022, Erik Blachford notified the Board of his resignation as a Class I
director and from the Compensation and NCGR Committees, effective February 8, 2022. Mr.
Blachford's resignation is not the result of any disagreement with the Company.

On February 7, 2022, the Board increased its size from seven to nine directors and appointed
Angel L. Mendez and Jonathan Mildenhall to the Board.

### CEO Transition; Executive Chair
On February 7, 2022, the Board appointed **Barry McCarthy as Chief Executive Officer and
President**, succeeding **John Foley**, the Company's Co-Founder and former CEO, effective
February 9, 2022. Mr. Foley was appointed **Executive Chair** of the Board. Mr. McCarthy was
also appointed to the Board.

Mr. McCarthy, 68, most recently served as Spotify's Chief Financial Officer from 2015 to
January 2020. From 1999 to 2010, he served as CFO and Principal Accounting Officer of Netflix,
Inc. He has served on the boards of Spotify and Instacart, and previously served on the boards
of Chegg, Eventbrite, MSD Acquisition Corp, Pandora, and Rent the Runway.

Under his Offer Letter, Mr. McCarthy is eligible for: (i) an annual base salary of $1,000,000,
(ii) a stock option award to purchase 8,000,000 shares of Class A Common Stock, (iii) up to
$150,000 relocation reimbursement, and (iv) up to $30,000 legal cost reimbursement.

### President Transition
In connection with Mr. McCarthy's appointment as President, **William Lynch** will transition
from this position to a non-executive director on the Board.
