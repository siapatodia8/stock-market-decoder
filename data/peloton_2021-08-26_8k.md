# Peloton Interactive, Inc. — Form 8-K
**Date of Report:** August 26, 2021

## Item 2.02 — Results of Operations and Financial Condition

On August 26, 2021, Peloton Interactive, Inc. ("Peloton" or the "Company") will hold a
conference call regarding its financial results for the year ended June 30, 2021. Peloton also
issued a letter to its stockholders announcing its financial results for the year ended June
30, 2021 (the "Shareholder Letter"). A copy of the Shareholder Letter is furnished as Exhibit
99.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K.

Peloton is making reference to non-GAAP financial information in both the Shareholder Letter
and the conference call. A reconciliation of GAAP to non-GAAP results is provided in the
Shareholder Letter.

Peloton uses its Investor Relations website, Peloton's Twitter feed (@onepeloton), John
Foley's Twitter feed (@keylargofoley), and the Peloton Press Newsroom as means of disclosing
material non-public information under Regulation FD.
